OUTPOST II
DIVIDED DESTINY — MODERN REMAKE
Year 2435. The colony ship Conestoga carried humanity's last million survivors to New Terra after Vulcan's Hammer obliterated Earth. But a secret terraforming experiment has produced a deadly mutation — a microbe that consumes all organic matter, spreading inexorably across the planet. The colonists call it the Blight. Eden and Plymouth, the two rival colonies, have precious little time to build a starship and escape. This is the story of Axen Moon and Emma Burke.
